## RESEARCH LABORATORY SPECIFICATIONS

<div class="pixel-box">

The **Pozzo Research Laboratory** is located in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the University of Washington. Our work focuses on:

**▓ SELF-ASSEMBLY CONTROL**
Development and control of self-assembly in soft materials

**▓ AI-DRIVEN DISCOVERY**
High-throughput materials discovery using AI and laboratory automation

**▓ ADVANCED CHARACTERIZATION**
Small-angle X-ray and neutron scattering (SAXS/SANS)

**▓ FUNCTIONAL NANOMATERIALS**
Energy storage and medical applications

**▓ OPEN-SOURCE AUTOMATION**
Tools for autonomous experimentation

</div>

<div class="pixel-box">

## RECRUITMENT PROTOCOLS

<div class="pixel-box">

**▓ GRADUATE STUDENTS**

If you're interested in joining our research group, please apply through the [UW Chemical Engineering](https://www.cheme.washington.edu/admissions) or [Molecular Engineering & Sciences (MolES)](https://www.moles.washington.edu/admissions-and-aid/) graduate programs.

In your application materials, mention your interest in the Pozzo research group and:
- AI/machine learning for materials
- Laboratory automation and robotics
- Soft matter and nanomaterials
- Autonomous experimentation

**▓ UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS**

UW undergraduates interested in research opportunities should contact Prof. Pozzo via email with:
- CV/Resume
- Transcript
- Brief statement of research interests

**▓ POSTDOCTORAL CANDIDATES**

Postdoctoral positions are occasionally available. Please send to dpozzo@uw.edu:
- CV
- Research statement
- Contact information for three references
