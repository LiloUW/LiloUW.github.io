## MISSION STATEMENT

I am the Vice Chair of Operations and Boeing-Roundhill Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the University of Washington, where I also hold an Adjunct Professorship in Human Centered Design & Engineering.

My research laboratory specializes in the development, measurement, and control of **self-assembly of soft materials** at the nano and micrometer scales, powered by **AI-driven autonomous experimentation** and **high-throughput laboratory automation**.
